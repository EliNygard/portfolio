About

I am based in Drammen, Norway. A perfect sized city, a quick train ride from the capital Oslo and a short way to the sea, the forest and the mountains.

After many years working in the film and television industry, I wanted to take on a new path in my career. Or as I see it, add on new knowledge. I have worked as a sound technician and sound designer on feature films, short films, tv-series, reality shows, documentaries and audio books. I love working with people towards a goal, as we do on a production. And I love project planning, as I have done as a coordinator at Minimalen International Short Film Festival for 7 years. I have always been interested in design and technology, and I am looking forward to bringing my present knowledge into this new direction in my career.

My focus is to become a steady, problem-solving developer who writes tidy code. I enjoy building software that is accessible and easy to use for all end-users, and I am very interested in what I as a developer can do for the environment. I am a social coder; I like to discuss issues and topics with colleagues and working in an open landscape.

When I am not at the computer, you can find me outside - no matter the weather, at a yoga class or digging in dirt doing gardening. I like keeping my body strong to support my mental well-being.
